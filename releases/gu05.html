No Borders Compilation
R.GU.COMP



"A compilation of electronic and experimental artists, most of whom have appeared on tapes from GU. The nice thing is that most of these folks want to explore sound rather than attack the listener. The start of side two is especially intriguing, featuring the aural landscapes of Stefan Tischler, Michael Chocolak, and Charles Cohen." Review by Robert Carlberg, c. 1988.

No Borders was originally released in 1987 by Generations Unlimited. Re-Mastered in 2014 by Thomas Dimuzio at GENCH Studios, San Francisco, CA.
