GENCON - New Dramatic Electronic Music
R.GU.GC1



"Conrad Schnitzler, one of the founding members of Tangerine Dream and one of the most prolific recordings artists on the planet, has teamed up [with] Gen Ken to produce Gen Con, an amalgam of these two electronic musical minds... it has a hard, experimental, free-wheeling edge with plenty of bizarre sounds, weird timbres, your archetypal hardcore electronic sonorities, and the more accessible strands of the German School of synthesizer bands. In other words, Gen Con runs parallel to the early, adventurous Tangerine Dream before the advent of sequencers and weenie space tunes. Music that stretches the imagination." -Review by Dean Suzuki c. 1988

Complete 60 minute version composed and recorded at Con's studio, Berlin, 1988, by Gen Ken Montgomery and Conrad Schnitzler.
